To start, I love reading books with my kid's. It's a simple, relaxing activity that brings us closer and make's us more intimate.


There are many, many benefits from kid's reading early or even just listening to their parent's read to them. Book's are very good at catching and holding young children's attention because of the rhyme and repetition. Reading help's them to learn emotion, be expressive and promote's brain development and imagination. I think people are unaware of the level of nurturing and reassurance it provide's because babies loving hearing familiar voice's.


A lot of kid's grow up frowning upon literacy, labelling it a boring subject but reading to them when they are very young and with an enthusiastic attitude will help them look at it in a positive way. Their level of comprehensions is also depends on if if they are paying attention - there listening skills, so its good practice in helping them take in to deatails too.
