Expanding young minds!

Why is reading so important?

As parents, we frequently hear about the advantages of reading to young children. If you stop to consider it, reading plays a significant role in our daily lives as adults. Early exposure to reading benefits language and cognitive development in babies and young children.


A story may be being read to your child, for sure. Alternatively, they can be getting a story told to them by one of their toys. However, as they are listening and understanding the story, their brain will conjure up a mental image of it. When someone reads to them or reads to them themselves, their imagination is sparked, and they create mental images that help them understand what they are reading or hearing.


You can include reading in your child's daily routine in a variety of ways. We don't always have time to curl up with a book. Many educational yet entertaining toys for newborns or toddlers have been released by toy companies like V-Tech or LeapFrog. Toys or activities that emphasise reading can be beneficial for kids as young as elementary school age.
